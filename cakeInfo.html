Sponge Cake

Sponge cake is a light cake made with eggs, flour and sugar.

Cheesecake

Cheesecake is a sweet dessert consisting of one or more layers. The main, and thickest layer, consists of a mixture of soft, fresh cheese, eggs, and sugar.
If there is a bottom layer, it often consists of a crust or base made from crushed cookies, graham crackers, pastry, or sometimes sponge cake.
It may be baked or unbaked.

Butter Cakes

A butter cake is a cake in which one of the main ingredients is butter.
Butter cake is baked with basic ingredients: butter, sugar, eggs, flour, and rising agents such as baking powder or baking soda.

Yeast Cakes

Yeast cakes are the oldest and are very similar to yeast bread.

Chiffon Cakes

A chiffon cake is a very light cake made with vegetable oil, eggs, sugar, flour, baking powder, and flavorings.

Chocolate Cakes

Chocolate cake or chocolate gâteau (from French: gâteau au chocolat) is a cake flavored with melted chocolate, cocoa powder, or both.

Coffee Cake

Coffee cake is cake flavored with or intended to be eaten with coffee.

Cupcakes

A small cake designed to serve one person.
